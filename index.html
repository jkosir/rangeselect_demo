Building your annual plan - step 1 of 3

Annual plan - Training periods

It's time to get planning. In this step you'll determine your season goals and distribute training blocks accordingly. Each training block starts with base period, the purpose of which is to build general foundation. Base period is followed by Build period, in which more specific abilities are improved, e.g. sprinting or climbing short hills. Build periods end with 1-2 weeks of top form.

For any racing goals you should plan your top form weeks around the most important races. If you don't have specific races in mind and would simply like to improve your abilities we recommend you stick with default distribution.
